What it checks

  • Wrong student name in a comment
  • Pronoun inconsistency (he/she mix)
  • Spelling typos and UK/US consistency
  • Grammar, punctuation & spacing
  • Negative or sensitive tone
  • Wordy or informal wording
  • First-person language (our, we, I)
  • Duplicate words (and and / the the)
  • Comment / level alignment
✍️

Style of feedback

Only comments that need attention are shown. Each row is specific to one student and report area, with a plain-English fix. Garbled or unclear text is skipped rather than guessed at.

🔒

Private by design

Everything runs in your browser. Your file is never sent to a server. No student data leaves your device.

✏️

Grammar, spelling & punctuation

  • Common spelling typos (e.g. recieve → receive)
  • UK vs US spelling mixed in the same document
  • Missing space after a full stop
  • Double spaces or space before punctuation
  • Duplicate words (and and, the the)
  • Contractions in reports (don’t → do not)
  • Repeated transition words (Furthermore… Furthermore)
  • Possible contradictions in the same comment
