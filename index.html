What it checks

  • Wrong student name (roster-based only)
  • Pronoun inconsistency within a comment
  • Spelling errors and UK/US consistency
  • Grammar: missing articles, agreement
  • Punctuation, spacing and hyphens
  • Sensitive or negative tone
  • First-person wording (we/our/I)
  • Wordiness and unclear language
  • Duplication and contradiction
  • Comment/level alignment
✍️

Style of feedback

Three clean tables matching the style of a human proofreader: grammar and consistency (with "Whole document" rows for patterns across the class), tone items to soften, and level alignment checks. Exact quotes, exact fixes.
